About Me

Hello! I'm Mohamed, a Mobile Developer based in Alexandria, Egypt

With a passion for creating seamless digital experiences, I specialize in developing high-performance mobile applications using Flutter and Native Android technologies. My journey in mobile development started during my computer science studies, and I've been hooked ever since.

I believe that great apps are a combination of intuitive design, clean code, and thoughtful user experience. That's why I focus not just on making apps work, but on making them delightful to use.

My expertise spans across various technologies including Kotlin, Java, Flutter, Firebase, and RESTful APIs. I'm committed to writing clean, maintainable code and following best practices in software development.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing knowledge with the developer community. I'm also an active member of AIESEC, where I participate in leadership development programs and international exchange opportunities.

I'm constantly learning and expanding my skill set to stay at the forefront of mobile development trends. My goal is to create applications that not only meet user needs but exceed their expectations with exceptional performance and design.
